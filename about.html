About us

Company History

And I've Always Said That was born out of turning favorite hobbies and day to day thoughts into an outlet for something to share with others

Through dedication and hard work, you're able to see the blog as you see it today, and there are no plans to stop there, everyday new ideas are constantly being generated into how the site and organization can be improved to give the audience the best experience we can offer.

Mission & Vision

Mission & Vision

And I've Always Said That looks to create an experience for it's audience to feel a like they're gossiping with their best friend with our Spill Your Guts segment, gaining valuable insight from what they can feel is a trusted source in regards to important lifestyle tips with our Health & Beauty segment, and having that first hand access into another person's thoughts and criticisms in regards to their favorite albums and songs with our Album Reviews & Rankings segment.

At The end of the day, the organization's goal is nothing more than to provide a fun, free, and creative site full of quality entertainment to last.
